About AlterSocks

In a short period of time, AlterSocks has gained high brand awareness and popularity on a Georgian market. Due to the high demand, company continuously opened new branches in Tbilisi, Batumi, Kutaisi, and also placed socks vending machine in Tbilisi International Airport. Now we are operating seven shops and exporting our products to more than ten countries. You can check our addresses here www.altersocks.ge/stores Our mission is to create colorful & high quality novelty socks that bring fun and joy into our customer’s everyday life.
